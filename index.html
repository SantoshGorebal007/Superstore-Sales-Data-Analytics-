Superstore Sales Data Analytics

Tableau Dashboard Project

Project Overview

+

This Superstore Sales Data Analytics project leverages Tableau and ETL/ELT methods to analyze over 8,500 sales records. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales performance, regional trends, monthly growth, and customer behavior, helping drive business decisions and strategy.

Dashboard Features

+

The dashboard includes:

  • Pie Chart: Sales distribution by region
  • Line Chart: Sales trends over months
  • Scatter Plot: Relationship between sales and other variables
  • Bar Chart: Regional sales amounts
  • Exported images and video preview

Sales Highlights

+
  • Analyzed over 8,500 sales records from Superstore dataset
  • Identified top-performing regions and months
  • Visualized sales growth and trends
  • Highlighted relationships between sales and other business metrics

Data Cleaning & Methods

+

Used ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) and ELT (Extract, Load, Transform) methods for cleaning and preparing the sales data. Ensured data quality and consistency for accurate analysis and visualization.

Regional & Monthly Analysis

+

Explore how different regions and months contribute to overall sales performance. Identify high-performing areas and seasonal trends to inform business strategy and growth opportunities.
